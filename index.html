Airbnb Bedroom Price Visualization

Apps like Airbnb have given every day people the opportunity to monetize their living spaces through vacation renting. Breaking into the market requires insights on area, number of bedrooms, and seasonal peaks in the area. Review this project to see the visualization of a 2016 Airbnb public listings in Washington state dataset that reveals several insights useful to those breaking into the market.

Warrior Cats Series Gender Analysis

A popular children's fantasy series, Warrior Cats by Erin Hunter features a large cast of sentient cats that tackle the struggle of living in a forest environment. This project involved extracting over 40 PDFs, extensive data cleaning, and the creation of an interactive dashboard allowing detailed filtering and analysis.

Shark Attacks Analysis

From dirty dataset cleaning in MySQL to final visualization in Tableau, this Shark Attack project analyzes over 1000 shark attacks and provides insights into where, what species, and fatalities associated with each shark attack.
